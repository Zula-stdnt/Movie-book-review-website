WALL-E

Synopsis

In the 29th century, WALL-E (Waste Allocation Load Lifter Earth-Class), a small waste-collecting robot, inadvertently embarks on a space journey that will decide the fate of humanity. Along the way, he meets sleek probe robot EVE and discovers a newfound purpose.

Notable Aspects:

  • First 40 minutes contain almost no dialogue
  • Won the Academy Award for Best Animated Feature
  • Environmental themes about consumerism and sustainability
