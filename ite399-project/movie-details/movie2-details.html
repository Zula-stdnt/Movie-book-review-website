The Shawshank Redemption

Synopsis

Andy Dufresne (Tim Robbins), a banker wrongly convicted of murder, endures life in Shawshank prison. With the help of fellow inmate Ellis "Red" Redding (Morgan Freeman), he finds hope and a path to redemption.
