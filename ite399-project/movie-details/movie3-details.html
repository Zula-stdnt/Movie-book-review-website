The Dark Knight

Synopsis

Batman (Christian Bale) teams with Commissioner Gordon (Gary Oldman) and DA Harvey Dent (Aaron Eckhart) to dismantle organized crime in Gotham, but their efforts are derailed by the anarchic Joker (Heath Ledger in an Oscar-winning performance).
