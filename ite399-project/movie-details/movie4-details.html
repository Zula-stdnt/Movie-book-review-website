Pulp Fiction

Synopsis

Quentin Tarantino's masterpiece weaves interconnected stories of two hitmen (John Travolta and Samuel L. Jackson), a boxer (Bruce Willis), and a mob wife (Uma Thurman) in a non-linear crime saga filled with dark humor and iconic dialogue.
